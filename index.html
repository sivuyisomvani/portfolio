About Me

I'm a Cape Town-based developer with a BSc in Applied Statistics from the University of Cape Town. My journey in tech began during my university years, where I discovered my passion for using data to solve real-world problems.

With over 3 years of professional experience, I've worked on various projects ranging from data analysis and visualization to full-stack web development. I'm particularly interested in the intersection of data science and web technologies, creating interactive and insightful data-driven applications.

When I'm not coding, you can find me hiking Table Mountain, exploring new coffee shops, or contributing to open-source projects. I'm always eager to learn new technologies and take on challenging projects.
